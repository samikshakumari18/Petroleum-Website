Achievements

Ziayee group of companies is a registered company in Afghanistan. In year 2003 ZG started its performance as a legal registered company with the license no (1601-08) recieved from Ministry of Commerce and Industries of Afghanistan and Afghanistan Chamber of Commerce & Industries (ACCI) as well. ZG started its carrier from Balkh province under strong leadership of Abdul Momen Ziayee (CEO) and Abdul Mujeeb Ziayee ( Vice president). ZG started carrier with great potential and hardworking team staff to reach the goal specified by ZG vision. ZG did limit itself in a small area, so it started to open new branches in other provinces and cities in afghanistan. Now ZG has its branches in Hairatan, Kabul, Herat, Kondoz, Nangarhar, Kandahar and Aqina border. In first step we widened our domestic branchesh as mentioned above. In second stepwe looked forward to open new branches abroad. Now, ZG has opened its branches in Kazakhstan, Turkey and Russia. We are now involved in trade market of several countries in several sectors. ZG is performing mainly in oil & gas sector. We have been trading in this sector since 2003 with Russia, Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan, Kuwait and so on. Now, ZG has one of the largest oil & gas installations in Afghanistan. Our installations are located in Hairatan and Aqina border cities. Our installation in Hairatan has huge capacity of oil storage as well as gas storage with updated equipments amd experienced staff. Our pump stations in Mazar-e-sharif city has huge share of supplying fuel & gas in this city. Our pump station in Tashqorghan highway and in Andkhoy city is also delivering best service for public. We care best about our pump stations to deliver the best. Huge amount of Mazar city fuel & gas is supplied by ZG installions and distributed to local traders as well. Now, ZG is covering other big cities by its quality and best service. We are also active in other sectors such as nutritive goods import and supply, flour import, dry fruit export and so on. Now we are concentrating on international level performance and work with our global partners. We look forward to deliver best in the best manner.
