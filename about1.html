President Message

Ziayee Group as one of the leading companieafs in the country encourages the spirit of hardworking and great quality in every industry. With this view ZG is putting its effort to spread this spirit firstly among its own employees then all over the country and globe. Our company looks wider and different to the future of global economy. We have entered to the new era of global economy, but it shall not be our stop point. We will grow with the growth of global economy and we shall go further to share our contribution to the global economy and reach to a world class standard. We would like to work with world class companies with world class qualities. We would like to continue our co-operation and contribution with our global partners. Certainly, we are planning to widen our sectors and work in different sectors. We will add to our branches all over the country and the world. We will welcome the new partners as well to work with. ZG has been working with several companies in Turkey since 2011 in different sectors to import goods from Turkey. ZG has also entered to a partnership with flour company in Kazakhstan and has imported big amounts of flour and other goods from Kazakhstan. We also work on nutrition sector, as we have imported nutritive goods to Afghanistan from Turkey, China and Norway. With this view to our business we are launching new branches and new products for ZG. We will increase and spread our business in every sector. We want to use update technology in our business to make it more professional, available and convinient to all. Our general vision for ZG is to spread business in society in the best and standard way possible. Providing employment opportinities for young generation and using experience of experts to make ZG in top is our priority, for the sake of delivering best to the society.
